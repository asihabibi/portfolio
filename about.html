About Me

Asiye Habibi

Jounior Frontend Developer

Greetings! I'm Asiye, and I have a story to share.

I was born in the picturesque city of Kerman, Iran, back in 1983. Life has blessed me with the role of being a loving mother to two incredible boys, who constantly inspire me to reach for the stars.

My journey has taken me into the vast realm of astrophysics, where I earned my Ph.D. Today, I proudly serve as a postdoctoral researcher at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad. My focus? The mesmerizing mysteries of galactic dynamics and the intricate formations and structures that shape our universe. Together with my brilliant team, we delve into these cosmic wonders, utilizing the power of simulations.

Python is our trusty companion as we embark on these cosmic voyages, and we use GALAXY and RAMSES codes to simulate the galaxies.

But my passion doesn't end there. I've found another universe to explore - the world of web development. Enthusiastically, I embarked on a journey, enrolling in a junior frontend course. Here, I've been honing my skills in HTML, JavaScript, and React. This world of web coding has ignited a new flame within me, and I'm thrilled to embark on this exciting journey.

And so, this website you're exploring right now is my first creation in this digital cosmos - a space where I can share my passions, my discoveries, and connect with fellow explorers of the digital frontier.

Thank you for joining me on this cosmic journey. Together, we'll reach for the stars and code our own constellations in the boundless galaxy of the web.
